Oatmeal Pancakes --- High Protein Edition

Link to Home

Great pancakes that are easy to eat and pack tons of nutrition

If you want to get swol, well here are some nice pancakges you can make.

Pancake mix ingredients

  • 500g cottage cheese
  • 500g oatmeal
  • 2 cups egg whites
  • 1tsp vanilla

Additional ingredients

  • maple syrup
  • blueberries
  • can sugar
  • fried, crispy chicken!

Directions!

  1. blend together ingredients for panckage mix until smooth
  2. using a quarter cup, plop mix onto griddle. Feel free to toss some blueberries in
  3. cook em up
  4. serve and enjoy!
