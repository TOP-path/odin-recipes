Bomb Lasagna Recipe

Link to Home

This Lasagna is simply the best!

Only the best lasagnas will use these unique (kind of) set of ingredients purely luscious and delictible I dare say. Enjoy!

  • 1 lb sweet italian sausage from nonne
  • 0.75 lb lean ground beef
  • 0.5 cup minced onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 28oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6.5oz cans tomato paste
  • 0.5 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tbsps chopped parsley
  • 1.5 tsps dried basil
  • 1.5 tsps salt
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 0.5 tsp fennel seeds
  • 0.25 tsp ground pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 0.75 lb mozzarella cheese sliced
  • 0.75 cup grated Parmesano Regianno cheese

Directions!

  1. gather your ingredients
  2. cook the sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a Dutch oven until browned
  3. Stir in crused tomatoes, sauce, paste and water. Season! Now simmer for 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, and cool noodles for 8-19 mins. Rinse with cold water
  5. Combine ricotta cheese with egg, 2 tbsp parsley and 1/2 tsp salt
  6. preheat oven to 375F (190C)
  7. assemble your lasagna
  8. bake in oven for 25 mins with foil on top. Remove foil and bake for additional 25 mins
  9. let lasagna rest for 10 minutes. It's worked hard!
